From May 21 to 25, 2026, moers festival brings five days of jazz, avant-garde, improvisation and new music to Moers. Around 200 artists from more than 20 nations will perform downtown, on Kastellplatz and at many other venues.

With the app, your festival is always with you:

- Browse the program by day, time and venue
- Save events and build your personal schedule
- Find information about artists, venues, the festival village, travel and services
- Keep program data available offline
- Get push notifications for changes, specials and important updates

Whether you are heading to Bühne Kastellplatz, the castle, churches, Peschkenhaus, Bollwerk, the skate park or Die Röhre: the moers festival app guides you through five festival days, spontaneous moersify concerts, workshops, discussions and the festival village.
