Welcome to the official moers festival app for the 55th festival, running from May 21 to May 25. This app is the perfect companion for your festival experience and provides you with all the important information about the program.
Download the entire program so you can access it anytime, anywhere. Stay up to date on all venues and events, and never miss a performance that interests you again. With push notifications, you’ll always be informed about last-minute program changes, specials, and severe weather alerts.

Under the theme “…like in a fairy tale,” the moers festival 2026 explores the power of storytelling. Fairy tales are not merely reduced to the great European tradition, but are understood as an artistic means of grappling with the complexity—and, unfortunately, the brutality—of our times. This theme is interpreted in a radically artistic way through two large-scale semi-staged productions: Gellert Szabo’s Ideal Orchestra and the Chroma Kollektiv’s music theater piece “Märchenhafte Klangschaften” translate the search for truth and the escape into fiction into powerful soundscapes.
